Enhanced Scroll Effects

This updated design ensures the images are larger than their containers to prevent white gaps when tilting during scroll animations.

The key changes include making images 120% of container size, centering them, and using higher resolution source images.

How It Works

The enhanced design addresses the white gap issue:

  • Images are now 120% the size of their containers
  • Images are absolutely positioned and centered
  • Higher resolution source images are used
  • The right image is positioned further to the right
  • Maintained all scroll interaction effects

Try scrolling to see the enhanced tilt effect without any white gaps appearing around the images.

The images will now tilt dramatically as you scroll, creating a more engaging visual effect without any distracting background showing through.
